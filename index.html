About

I’m a postdoctoral researcher at the University of Alberta (Feb 2023–Dec 2026), working with Prof. Matina Kalcounis-Rueppell and Prof. Erin Bayne. My work focuses on large-scale acoustic monitoring, and ecological modelling, with applications to conservation and management. I am generally interested in Bayesian inference and non-invasive monitoring methods; get in touch if you would like to work together!

Interesting links

I have been recently working with Apoorv Saini, a great PhD Student in the Bioacoustic Unit. Amongst other things, we are working on an experiment that we co-designed with the Conklin Métis community to get an idea of whether (and how much) industrial noise affects sound propagation, specifically of Moose calls; we had two journalists, Vince from the Fort McMurray Today and Jasmeen from CBC, joining us for one day in the field.

AviaNZ is an open source piece of software from one of my PhD advisors, Stephen Marsland, as well as his students and collaborators.
